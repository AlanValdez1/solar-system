Welcome to our Solar System

In the beginning there was nothing, then there was that thing called the Big Bang theory which explains how all the stars and planets came to be. Which brings us to here. Our solar system. Currently has 8 planets, 4 of which are terrestrial and the other 4 being gas giants. The main 8 are separated by an asteroid belt and there is another belt just past Neptune that is much larger and known as the Kuiper Belt. There are also 5 dwarf planets and something over 200 moons.
